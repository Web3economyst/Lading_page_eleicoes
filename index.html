Statistical Evaluation of Electoral Polling Accuracy

A quantitative assessment of polling institute performance, systematic bias patterns, and forecasting calibration in Brazilian elections.

Download Paper (PDF) View Methodology

Abstract

This study evaluates the predictive performance of major Brazilian polling institutes across multiple electoral cycles. Using forecast error decomposition, bias estimation, and volatility-adjusted calibration models, we assess systematic deviations between pre-election surveys and official results.

Methodological Framework

Bias Estimation

Mean error and directional bias across institutes and election years.

Forecast Calibration

Adjustment models incorporating volatility and historical deviation.

Error Decomposition

Systematic vs. random components of polling error.
